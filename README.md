## Why This List Exists

Your dog was with you for 12 years. It saw you graduate, break up, get your first job, move cities, fall in love again. It didn't understand a word you said but it understood everything you felt.

Then one day it's gone.

You have 3,000 photos, 200 videos, and hundreds of messages where you told friends about it. All that data sits in your phone doing nothing.

**What if AI could distill your pet's personality from that data — its quirks, its habits, what made it happy, what scared it, how it showed love?**

Not a chatbot pretending to be your dog. Not an AI-generated photo of it wearing a hat. A real distillation of who it was — so you never forget, and so you can revisit it on the days when you miss it most.

This list tracks AI agent skills that do something meaningful with the bond between humans and their pets.

## What Counts as a Pet Skill?

### Must have:

**✅ Pets at the center** — The skill must be meaningfully about the human-pet relationship. Not a generic photo organizer that happens to work on pet photos.

**✅ Real utility or emotional value** — Either it helps you take better care of a living pet, or it helps you preserve the memory of one that's gone.

### Does NOT count:

❌ Generic AI photo generators ("make my dog a pirate")
❌ Novelty pet translators (fake bark-to-English)
❌ Pet product recommendation engines (glorified ads)
❌ Skills that have nothing specifically to do with pets

## Data Sources for Pet Skills

If you're building a pet skill, these are the data types that matter:

| Data Type | Where It Lives | What It Reveals |
|---|---|---|
| Photos | Camera roll, Google Photos, iCloud | Appearance changes, favorite spots, who they're with, mood |
| Videos | Camera roll, social media | Movement patterns, vocalizations, reactions, play style |
| Messages | WeChat, WhatsApp, iMessage, texts | How you talk about your pet, milestones you shared, concerns |
| Social posts | Instagram, 小红书, Facebook, X | Public memories, comments from others who knew your pet |
| Vet records | Vet clinic apps, paper records | Health timeline, medications, conditions, weight changes |
| Purchase history | Amazon, Taobao, pet store receipts | Diet changes, toy preferences, seasonal needs |
| Calendar | Google Calendar, Apple Calendar | Walk schedules, vet appointments, grooming, boarding |
| Smart home | Cameras, Furbo, pet doors | Behavior when you're away, sleep patterns, activity levels |
